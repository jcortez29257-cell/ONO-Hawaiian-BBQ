ONO Hawaiian

Get ready to take your taste buds on a tropical adventure! At UNO Hawaiian, we serve up island-inspired favorites like juicy kalua pork, sweet and savory teriyaki chicken, and tender garlic shrimp. Don’t forget our fresh poke bowls, crispy spam musubi, and hearty plate lunches that come with fluffy rice and creamy mac salad. Feeling something sweet? Try our tropical desserts and refreshing drinks that scream aloha! Every dish is made with fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a sprinkle of island love, so whether you’re here for a quick bite or a full feast, UNO Hawaiian makes every meal fun, flavorful, and totally unforgettable.
